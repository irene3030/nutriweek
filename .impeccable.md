## Design Context

### Users
Parents of a ~12 month old baby practicing BLW (Baby-Led Weaning). Used primarily on mobile during the week to plan and track meals. Context: kitchen counter, quick glances, one hand free. The job: plan the week's meals efficiently and log what actually got eaten.

### Brand Personality
Calm, engineering, practical. Three words: **precise, functional, grounded**.

### Aesthetic Direction
Blueprint/technical: engineering grid paper background, hard-edged flat shadows (offset, no blur), border-radius capped at 4px. Blueprint blue (#1A406B) as primary, cyan (#00BFFF) as technical/AI accent, caldero orange (#E67E22) as action color. Light theme (cream #FDFCF5). Spanish UI.

**Anti-references**: Not a fitness app (no progress rings, motivational copy, gradient fills). Not baby-themed (no pastels, no rounded bubbly type, no pink). Not a wellness app (no soft glows, no glassmorphism). Not generic SaaS (no hero metrics, no card grid templates).

### Typography
- **UI body**: Barlow (400/500/600/700) — DIN-inspired grotesque, engineering/industrial DNA
- **Display headings**: Barlow Condensed (600/700) — use `font-display font-bold tracking-tight` on section headings, screen titles, wordmark
- **Data/mono**: JetBrains Mono — use `.data-value` CSS class or `font-mono` for numeric/technical values

### Design Principles
1. **Density over decoration** — every pixel earns its place; no decorative elements that don't carry information
2. **Engineering clarity** — grids, hard edges, monospace data values; this is a tool, not a brand experience
3. **Calm hierarchy** — subdued palette with one sharp accent; never compete for attention, just organize it
4. **Mobile-first utility** — designed for one-hand use on a phone; touch targets, thumb zones, fast access
5. **Neutral intelligence** — AI features present but not celebrated; functional, not theatrical
